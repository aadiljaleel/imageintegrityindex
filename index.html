A Declaration of Editing

The IÂ³ score is a standardized artist's statement that provides transparent insight into your creative process.

Global Adjustment (G)

Measures "darkroom-style" edits like color correction, exposure, contrast, and tone adjustments.

G0 (Heavy edits) G100 (Minimal edits)

Structural Manipulation (S)

Measures content changes like object removal, additions, composites, and AI-generated elements.

S0 (Heavy manipulation) S100 (No manipulation)
